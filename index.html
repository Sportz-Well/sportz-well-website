Attendance, weekly skill scores (Physical, Technical, Mental), match statistics, and subjective remarks — all in one app.

About the Founder

Built by someone who lived it.

Sportz-Well was not built by a generalist software agency. It was architected by Jitendra Jagdale — a Mumbai cricketer who spent decades watching talented young players get lost in the system because no one was tracking their development properly.

Coaches were using WhatsApp groups and paper registers. Parents had no visibility into their child's progress. Academies had no data. SWPI was built to fix exactly that.

Jitendra played the U-17 Satkar Trophy under the captaincy of Sachin Tendulkar — and later became an MCA Match Observer, giving him institutional insight into what elite cricket development looks like at every level.
